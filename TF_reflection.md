---

PHASE 1 - UML Design (Plan Mode):

4 main classes we have:

- **Owner:** provides the pet owner’s information and identifies all the pets they have.

- **Pet:** provides pet’s information and identifies pet’s care tasks.

- **Task:** provides information about a single care activity.

- **Scheduler:** organizes tasks into a daily plan.
